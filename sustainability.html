Commitments

What We Stand For

eco

Ethically Sourced Materials

All linen is sourced from certified farms in France and Belgium — grown without synthetic pesticides, respecting the soil that sustains it. Our silk comes exclusively from OEKO-TEX certified mills in India, where no harmful chemicals enter the process at any stage. We trace every metre of fabric back to its origin, and we share that story with you.

handshake

Artisan Partnerships

We work directly with small workshops in Jaipur and Tamil Nadu, bypassing intermediaries to pay above fair-trade wages to every craftsperson. We document every step of our supply chain and publish it openly, because transparency is not optional — it is foundational. Our artisan partners are collaborators, not vendors.

inventory_2

Minimal Packaging

Every order ships in recycled kraft paper, secured with cotton ribbon and accompanied by a handwritten note. We eliminated plastic from all packaging in 2022, and our tissue paper is sourced from FSC-certified forests. The unboxing experience is designed to feel like a gift — to you, and to the planet.

The L'ÉTÉ Thrift Programme

Give your pre-loved pieces a second life — and earn credit for your next chapter.

We believe luxury shouldn't be disposable. The L'ÉTÉ Thrift Programme lets you send back pieces you've outgrown, and in return, you receive store credit to invest in your next wardrobe addition. Every returned piece is inspected, cleaned, and either resold at a reduced price or repurposed into something new.

Thrifted pieces carry the stories of the women who wore them — and that's what makes them extraordinary.

How It Works

  1. 1

    Submit your piece

    Fill in our online form with photos and condition notes. We'll review within 48 hours.

  2. 2

    We review & collect

    We arrange a free pickup if your piece qualifies. No trips to the post office needed.

  3. 3

    Earn store credit

    Receive 20–40% of the original price as store credit, applied instantly to your account.

Materials

Every Thread Tells a Story

grass

French Linen

Linum usitatissimum

Breathable, biodegradable, and grown without irrigation in the flax fields of Normandy and Brittany. Our linen is unbleached at source, retaining its natural texture. It gets softer with every wash, aging into something more beautiful than it began.

water_drop

Sandwashed Silk

Bombyx mori · OEKO-TEX Certified

OEKO-TEX certified from start to finish, our sandwashed silk is ethically produced by families in Karnataka with decades of sericulture expertise. Naturally temperature-regulating, it keeps you cool in the heat and warm when the evening turns. Luxurious without apology.

spa

Organic Cotton

Gossypium hirsutum · GOTS Certified

GOTS certified and free of synthetic pesticides. Harvested by smallholder farmers across Vidarbha and Gujarat — farmers we know by name, farms we visit in person. Our organic cotton is a commitment to the land and the people who tend it with such care.
